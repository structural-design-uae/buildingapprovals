## Image Requirements:
- Format: PNG with transparent background (preferred) or white background
- Size: Minimum 200x200px, Maximum 500x500px
- Aspect ratio: Square or close to square works best
- Quality: High resolution, clear and professional

## Download Tips:
- Look for "Press" or "Media" sections on official websites for high-quality logos
- Use Google Images with "filetype:png" and site-specific searches
- Ensure you have the right to use these logos (they're typically available for representation purposes)
